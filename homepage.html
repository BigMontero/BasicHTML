Digital Marketing refers to the use of digital channels, platforms, and technologies to promote or sell products and services to consumers and businesses. It is a broad field that includes a wide range of strategies and tactics aimed at reaching and engagingtarget audiences online. We help startups and enterprises build modern web and mobile experiences.

Search Engine Optimization
Improving website visibility on search engines like Google.
Involves on-page, off-page, and technical SEO
Content Marketing
Creating and distributing content to attract and retain a clearly defined audience.
Includes blogs, videos, infographics, ebooks, webinars, etc.
Social Media Marketi
Using platforms like Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, TikTok to promote content and engage with audiences.
Includes organic posting and paid ads.
Email Marketing
Sending targeted emails to nurture leads, retain customers, and drive sales.
Often personalized and automated.
Pay-Per-Click Advertising
Paid ads on search engines (like Google Ads) and social platforms.
Advertisers pay only when someone clicks on their ad.
Affiliate Marketing
Promoting products or services through third parties (affiliates) who earn a commission for each sale or lead.
Influencer Marketing
Collaborating with social media influencers to promote products to their followers.
Online Public Relations
Managing brand reputation and media presence through digital platforms.
Mobile Marketing
Marketing via mobile devices through SMS, in-app advertising, mobile-friendly websites, and push notifications.
Analytics & Data
Tracking performance using tools like Google Analytics, social insights, and CRM data to optimize marketing efforts.
