Call Of Duty:Mobile

Call of Duty: Mobile is a free-to-play first-person shooter developed by Tencent Games and published by Activision. Released on October 1, 2019, it brings the iconic Call of Duty experience to mobile devices, featuring a blend of classic and new content tailored for on-the-go gaming. Call of Duty: Mobile is available on both Android and iOS platforms. It requires at least 3GB of RAM and Android 9 or iOS 10 to run smoothly .
