Quotation

A quotation is a repetition of someone else's statement or thoughts. It is often used in writing to provide evidence, illustrate ideas, or acknowledge the origin of certain ideas or expressions.

Life is one big road with lots of signs. So when you riding through the ruts, don't complicate your mind. Flee from hate, mischief and jealousy. Don't bury your thoughts, put your vision to reality. Wake Up and Live!

Short Quotes

Short quotes are brief expressions—usually one sentence or a short phrase—that capture a powerful idea, emotion, or insight. Because of their brevity, they are easy to remember and often used for motivation, reflection, or inspiration.

Steve Jobs: Stay hungry, stay foolish.

Abbreviation

Apple Inc. was founded on April 1, 1976 by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne.
