Background

Sarkodie was born in Tema, Ghana, to a mother from Kwahu and a father from Konongo. He grew up in Tema Community 7 and attended Tema Methodist Day Senior High School.

His interest in music began at an early age, influenced by both Ghanaian highlife and international hip-hop artists. He started writing his own lyrics while still in school, honing his craft through local rap battles and performances.

Music Career

Sarkodie commanding the stage

How He Started

Sarkodie's professional music career began in 2005 when he started performing at local shows and collaborating with other underground artists in Tema and Accra.

His breakthrough came in 2009 when he was featured on the song "Baby" by Ghanaian musician Castro, which became a nationwide hit. This exposure led to the release of his debut album "Makye" later that year.

"I used to rap for free, just for the love of it. My breakthrough came when I decided to be authentically Ghanaian in my music - rapping in Twi changed everything for me."
